Welcome To Carson Motors

Carson Motors is an American automaker headquartered in Dallas, Texas. The company started out as Carson & Bennett and they produced their first car in the 1930s, the infamous Carson & Bennett Grand Marais. The Grand Marais has a rich and storied history as its speed and maneuverability quickly made it the vehicle of choice for both the cops and the mafia. Following the Grand Marais, another legendary vehicle was born, the Carson & Bennett Hot Rod Coupe, a defining point in the American Hot Rod culture. After the Coupe, the company changed its name to Carson. It was in the 1960’s that Carson decided to get into the Competition segment with the “24 Endurance Championship” and yet another American legend was born, the Carson 500GT with a 4.2L V8 and 500 HP. This firmly cemented Carson’s place as a powerhouse in competition cars. In the 1970s began Carson’s long standing rivalry with Hunter when the latter introduced cars like the Cavalry, Vegas and Mesquite. However, Carson was not one to back down and their efforts to design 3 slick muscle cars led to the creation of the Carson Fastback, the Carson Opus and the one and only, Carson Annihilator.
